Our Story


Since launching in 2019, ZUS Coffee has grown by leaps & bounds; and it would not have been possible without all of you.


From the beginning, our dream was always to deliver specialty coffee as a daily necessity for ALL to enjoy. For many, specialty coffee is often seen as a luxury, something you treat yourself to on special occasions. We wanted to change this perception.ZUS Coffee is a cup of clean, consistent & well-handled coffee. With the best quality ingredients, high-level coffee brewing technology and innovative business model, we are evolving the concept of coffee consumption to make specialty coffee affordable for everyone, everyday.With over 8.8 million cups delivered to date, we only have our ZUSsies to thank for all our achievements thus far. Your amazing loyalty and support are what motivated us to keep our promise to you and ensure that ZUS Coffee is always a Necessity, not a Luxury.
